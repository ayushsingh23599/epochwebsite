About Us

EPOCH is the Literary and Cultural Society of Jamia Hamdard, Delhi.

Founded in 2019, EPOCH aims to create and facilitate an environment for enhancing innate potential, ensure holistic development, and contribute to the multicultural makeup of the students through participation in various cultural and literary events.

Holistic Development

One of our primary objectives is to ensure holistic development of the students from across the University and inculcate cultural awareness among the members and in communities.

Finest Opportunities

Epoch provides the finest and inordinate opportunities and exposure to its members so that they explore their talents and potentialities in the field of Music, Theatre, Literary, Dance, and Fine arts.

Top-Notch Collaborations

To impart different skills, self-reliance, and humanitarianism among the youth to build a better world, Epoch is actively collaborated with multiple global and national institutions, not for profit organisations, and college societies.

OUR VISION

To develop the hidden talents and soft skills of the student members of EPOCH Society and the sense of confidence in their respective fields of interest. The Society through its extensive activities thrives to create high moral, social, and cultural values within its members existentially.

To impart higher quality skills in all literary and art forms by undertaking various activities and workshops and provide a platform to the students to explore their potential.

What makes us stand out?

EPOCH is place where you can be passionate, express or enunciate, explore hidden talents, socialise better, develop membership, soft, and hard skills, and embrace the exposure along with interaction.EPOCH that makes it stand out.
